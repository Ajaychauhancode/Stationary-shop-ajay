Cash On Delivery (COD) Details About Jai Mata Di Stationary .

Cash on Delivery or COD is only applicable to orders within India.
The delivery executive will attempt a delivery thrice before returning the parcel to destination. In case of failed or fraudulent COD, the customer will not be allowed to order via COD again. Mango reserves the right to cancel a COD order if suspected as fraudulent or inauthentic on any grounds.
Our staff may contact you to confirm your COD order. Orders not confirmed by the customer within 3 days of placing an order, will be cancelled automatically on the 4th day.
We do not process COD orders to training institutes, college, school, hostels, cafe, restaurants, hotels and other such addresses. If you place a COD order with us, please make sure you provide a residential address. Customers are welcome to use our prepaid services to such addresses.
Returns are not applicable on personalised COD orders.
Orders above INR 2000 are not applicable for COD.

Order Cancellations

We will be able to accept a cancellation request any time before we ship your order. In case your credit card/debit card/bank account was already charged for the order, we shall process the refund immediately following the cancellation of the order, subject to deductions for any banking transaction charges incurred by us pursuant to your order and its cancellation.
Unfortunately, we cannot cancel orders once the shipment is out of our store for delivery. Personalised orders cannot be cancelled if we have started processing your order.
